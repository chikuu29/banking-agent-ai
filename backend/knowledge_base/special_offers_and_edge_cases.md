The bank's internal scoring system generates pre-approved offers based on:

| Factor | Weight | Logic |

## 7. Edge Cases & Exception Handling

### Case 1: Customer with Settled/Written-Off Account
- **Policy**: Auto-decline for unsecured products within 3 years of settlement
- **Exception**: If settlement was due to medical emergency (documented), manual override available with Branch Manager + Regional Head dual approval
- **Interest Rate Impact**: +2% above standard rate if exception is approved
- **Credit Card**: Secured credit card only (against FD collateral) with 12-month probation

### Case 2: Self-Employed with Irregular Income
- **Policy**: 2-year ITR averaging for income calculation
- **Exception**: If GST returns show stable monthly turnover for 18+ months, GST-based assessment allowed
- **Proof Required**: 24-month GST returns + CA certification of average monthly income
- **Eligible Products**: Personal loan up to ₹5L; MSME loan based on turnover

### Case 3: Applicant with Zero Credit History (New-to-Credit)
- **Policy**: Cannot get unsecured products without credit history
- **Exception Path**:
  1. Open a savings account and maintain for 6 months
  2. Take a secured credit card (against FD of ₹25,000+)
  3. After 6 months of credit card usage with 0 DPD, eligible for personal loan up to ₹1L
  4. After 12 months, full product suite becomes accessible
- **Alternative**: Salary account holders with 6+ months history treated as "bankable" even without bureau score

### Case 4: Foreign National (Non-NRI) Working in India
- **Policy**: Must have valid work visa (Employment Visa / Business Visa)
- **Eligible Products**: Savings Account (with KYC + visa copy), Credit Card (secured), Personal Loan (if employed at listed company for 2+ years)
- **Not Eligible**: Home Loan (cannot own property), Gold Loan, MSME Loan
- **Special KYC**: Passport + Visa + FRRO Registration + Address proof in India

### Case 5: Loan for Agricultural Land Purchase
- **Policy**: Regular home loan does not cover agricultural land
- **Exception**: If land is being converted to residential (NA permission obtained), home loan applicable
- **Alternative**: Agricultural loan under PSL category; max ₹5L per acre; interest rate: 7% p.a.
- **Special Scheme**: Farmer Credit Card for short-term crop loans at subsidized rates

### Case 6: Customer Requesting Loan Above Maximum Limit
- **Policy**: Products have defined maximum limits (e.g., personal loan ₹15L)
- **Exception**: HNI customers (relationship value > ₹1 Crore) can request higher limits with:
  - Additional collateral (property, FD, insurance policy)
  - Higher income documentation (last 3 years ITR showing > ₹25L p.a.)
  - Approval from Circle Head or Zonal Credit Committee
- **Risk Weight**: 150% for exceptions above standard limits

### Case 7: Joint Loan Application — Income Computation
- **Salaried + Salaried**: 100% of higher income + 50% of lower income considered
- **Salaried + Self-Employed**: 100% of salaried income + 75% of self-employed (based on ITR average)
- **Self-Employed + Self-Employed**: 75% of each income (based on ITR average of 2 years)
- **Retired + Working**: Pension counted at 100%; working spouse income at 100%
- **Maximum co-applicants**: 4 (for home loan); 2 (for personal loan — spouse only)

## 9. Regulatory Exceptions During Economic Events

### RBI Moratorium Provisions (for future economic disruptions)
- If RBI announces a moratorium (as in COVID-19), all term loan EMIs may be deferred
- During moratorium: Interest accrues on outstanding but no penal charges
- Post-moratorium: EMI recalculated with accrued interest spread over remaining tenure
- Borrower opt-in required (moratorium is not automatic)

### Natural Disaster Relief
- For areas declared under natural disaster (flood, earthquake, cyclone):
  - Loan moratorium: Up to 12 months (principal + interest deferment)
  - Restructuring: Tenure extension up to 5 years without NPA classification
  - Processing fee waiver on all new loans for disaster-affected borrowers
  - Enhanced home loan limit for reconstruction (additional 25% over standard limit)
  - Agriculture loans: Interest waiver for affected crop season

### Inflation-Linked Rate Adjustments
- If CPI inflation exceeds 6% for 3 consecutive months:
  - Bank may invoke annual rate review clause on fixed-rate loans
  - EBLR-linked loans automatically adjust with repo rate changes
  - FD rates revised quarterly to maintain positive real returns
  - Savings account rate remains floor at 3.50% (RBI minimum)
