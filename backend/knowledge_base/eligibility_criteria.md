## Product: SmartFlex Personal Loan (PL-001)

### Eligibility Matrix

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 21 – 58 years (at loan maturity, max 60 years) |
| Minimum Monthly Salary | ₹30,000 (net take-home) |
| Minimum Annual Income (Self-Employed) | ₹5,00,000 (based on ITR) |
| Employment Type | Salaried (min 1 year current employer) or Self-Employed (min 2 years) |
| Minimum Credit Score | 700 (650 considered for existing customers with 2+ year relationship) |
| Existing Loan Restriction | Maximum 2 active unsecured loans across all lenders |
| FOIR Limit | Total EMIs (including proposed) ≤ 50% of net monthly income |
| Citizenship | Indian Resident (NRIs not eligible for unsecured personal loans) |
| Income Proof | Salaried: 3-month salary slips + 6-month bank statement; Self-Employed: 2-year ITR + CA certificate |
| PAN/Aadhaar | Both mandatory |
| Work Experience | Minimum 12 months total; minimum 6 months at current employer |
| Employer Category | Listed companies, MNCs, Government, PSUs, reputed private firms (Category A/B) |

### Special Relaxations
- Government employees: Credit score relaxed to 650; FOIR extended to 55%
- Existing salary account holders (2+ years): Processing fee waived; credit score relaxed to 680
- Women applicants: 0.25% rate concession on approved rate

### Rejection Criteria (Auto-Decline)
- Credit score below 600
- DPD (Days Past Due) > 30 in last 12 months on any credit facility
- Existing write-off or settlement in credit history (last 3 years)
- Current FOIR exceeding 60% (even with relaxations)
- Employment in restricted industries (gambling, cryptocurrency trading, tobacco)

## Product: HomeDream Housing Loan (HL-001)

### Eligibility Matrix

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 23 – 65 years (at loan maturity, max 70 years) |
| Minimum Monthly Salary | ₹40,000 (net take-home) |
| Minimum Annual Income (Self-Employed) | ₹6,00,000 |
| Employment Type | Salaried (min 2 years total), Self-Employed (min 3 years ITR), Business Owner (min 3 years GSTR) |
| Minimum Credit Score | 650 (700 for loans above ₹50L) |
| Existing Loan Restriction | No restriction, but total FOIR considered |
| FOIR Limit | Total EMIs ≤ 65% of household net monthly income |
| Citizenship | Indian Resident, NRI (with specific NRI documentation) |
| Income Proof | Salaried: 6-month salary slips + 1-year bank statement; Self-Employed: 3-year ITR + Balance Sheet |
| PAN/Aadhaar | Both mandatory for all applicants and co-applicants |
| Work Experience | Salaried: Min 2 years total, 1 year current employer; Self-Employed: Min 3 years in same business |
| Property Location | Must be in bank-approved project or locality; rural/remote areas may need special approval |

### Co-Applicant Rules
- Spouse income can be combined for eligibility computation (co-applicant mandatory)
- Parent income can be added for applicants below 30 years
- Co-applicant must be a co-owner of the property if their income is being considered
- Maximum 3 co-applicants allowed on a single home loan application

### Special Relaxations
- Defence personnel: Credit score relaxed to 625; max tenure extended to 35 years
- Below-35 borrowers: Step-up EMI option with FOIR calculated on projected income
- Balance transfer customers: Processing fee waived if outstanding > ₹25L
- EWS/LIG (income < ₹6L): Interest subsidy under PMAY of 6.5% on first ₹6L of loan

### Rejection Criteria (Auto-Decline)
- Credit score below 550
- DPD > 60 in last 24 months
- Property in litigation or without clear title
- Builder blacklisted by bank or RERA-non-compliant
- Age + tenure exceeding 70 years (75 for select categories)

## Product: Platinum Rewards Credit Card (CC-001)

### Eligibility Matrix

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 21 – 60 years (add-on card: 18+ years) |
| Minimum Monthly Salary | ₹50,000 (net take-home) |
| Minimum Annual Income (Self-Employed) | ₹8,00,000 |
| Employment Type | Salaried (min 1 year), Self-Employed (min 2 years with stable income) |
| Minimum Credit Score | 720 |
| Existing Card Restriction | Max 5 active credit cards across all issuers |
| Total Credit Exposure | Proposed credit limit + existing limits ≤ 200% of annual income |
| Citizenship | Indian Resident |
| Income Proof | Salaried: Latest salary slip + Form 16; Self-Employed: Latest ITR |
| PAN/Aadhaar | PAN mandatory; Aadhaar for eKYC |
| Work Experience | Min 1 year at current employer (salaried) |
| Bureau Inquiries | Max 4 hard inquiries in last 6 months |

### Upgrade Path
- Silver → Gold: Annual spends ≥ ₹2L and credit score ≥ 700
- Gold → Platinum: Annual spends ≥ ₹3L and credit score ≥ 720 and income ≥ ₹6L p.a.
- Platinum → Signature: Annual spends ≥ ₹8L and credit score ≥ 750 and income ≥ ₹15L p.a.

### Rejection Criteria (Auto-Decline)
- Credit score below 680
- Outstanding settled/written-off account in last 5 years
- More than 2 credit card applications in last 30 days
- Current credit utilization > 80% across all cards

## Product: GoldSecure Gold Loan (GL-001)

### Eligibility Matrix

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 18 – 70 years |
| Minimum Monthly Salary | No minimum income requirement for loans < ₹3L |
| Minimum Annual Income (for loans > ₹3L) | ₹1,50,000 (basic income proof required) |
| Employment Type | Any (Salaried, Self-Employed, Housewife, Farmer, Retired, Student) |
| Minimum Credit Score | No minimum (secured by gold) |
| Existing Loan Restriction | No restriction |
| Citizenship | Indian Resident |
| Income Proof | Not required for loans < ₹3L; Self-declaration for ₹3L – ₹10L; Full proof for > ₹10L |
| PAN/Aadhaar | Aadhaar mandatory; PAN for loans > ₹2L |
| Gold Requirements | 18 – 24 carat purity; ornaments only (no coins, bars, or bullion) |
| LTV (Loan-to-Value) | Maximum 75% of gold market value |

### Special Features
- No credit score check — 100% approval rate (subject to gold quality)
- Widest age bracket of any product (18-70)
- Housewives and farmers eligible without income documentation
- Same-day disbursal (within 30 minutes at branch)

### Rejection Criteria
- Gold purity below 18 carat
- Stolen/pledged gold (verification against police records)
- Gold ornaments with religious significance may be declined at branch discretion
- Third-party gold (gold not owned by applicant) requires NOC from owner

## Product: BusinessPrime MSME Loan (BL-001)

### Eligibility Matrix

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Business Owner Age | 21 – 65 years |
| Business Vintage | Minimum 2 years of operations (3 years for manufacturing) |
| Annual Turnover | ₹10,00,000 – ₹250,00,00,000 (₹10L – ₹250 Crore) |
| Minimum Credit Score | 680 (business owner / promoter) |
| MSME Registration | Udyam Registration Certificate mandatory |
| GST Registration | Mandatory; regular filing (no gaps > 2 months in last 12 months) |
| Citizenship | Indian Resident (business registered in India) |
| Industry Type | Manufacturing, Trading, Services (excluded: real estate, gambling, speculative) |
| Income Proof | 2-year audited Balance Sheet + P&L; ITR of promoters; 12-month bank statement |
| PAN/Aadhaar | Both mandatory for business entity and all promoters/directors |
| Collateral | None for < ₹10L (CGTMSE); Property for ₹10L – ₹5Cr; additional collateral for > ₹2Cr |
| Personal Guarantee | Mandatory from all promoters/directors with >20% shareholding |

### Special Schemes
- MUDRA Loan (Shishu: < ₹50K, Kishore: ₹50K – ₹5L, Tarun: ₹5L – ₹10L): No collateral, subsidized rates
- Stand-Up India: For SC/ST/Women entrepreneurs; loan ₹10L – ₹1Cr; no collateral
- PMEGP: Subsidy of 15% – 35% on project cost for new manufacturing units
- ECLGS (Emergency Credit Line): Additional 20% of outstanding as of Feb 2020; fully guaranteed by Government

### Rejection Criteria
- CIBIL rank below 5 (for the business entity)
- Promoter/director on RBI defaulters list or wilful defaulter list
- Negative net worth of the business
- Business in restricted industry (arms, narcotics, real estate speculation)
- Outstanding statutory dues (GST, PF, ESI) exceeding ₹5L
- Any criminal proceedings against promoters related to financial fraud

## Product: WealthMax Mutual Fund (MF-001)

### Eligibility Matrix

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 18+ years (minor through guardian) |
| KYC | Full KYC (CKYC or KRA) mandatory |
| PAN | Mandatory for all mutual fund investments |
| Bank Account | Active savings/current account with the bank |
| Minimum Investment | SIP: ₹500/month; Lumpsum: ₹5,000 |
| Citizenship | Indian Resident, NRI (with select AMC restrictions) |
| Risk Profiling | Mandatory risk profiling questionnaire before first investment |
| Demat Account | Not mandatory (units held in statement of account); optional for demat holding |

### NRI Restrictions
- US/Canada-based NRIs: Cannot invest in most equity mutual funds (FATCA restrictions)
- NRIs from other countries: Allowed with KYC + NRE/NRO bank account
- ELSS not available for NRIs in some AMCs

### Rejection Criteria
- PAN not linked with KYC registry
- Failed risk profiling (flagged for investment suitability)
- Sanctioned country citizen (OFAC/UN sanctions list)
- Minor without valid guardian KYC
